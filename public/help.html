How to Use This App

Use this guide to search for a location, generate a climate risk report, and understand the information shown on the report page.

1. Search for a Location

Go to the Home page and type in a city or location, such as College Park. Then click the Generate Report button.

2. View the Climate Risk Report

The report page shows the location, risk score, current weather, temperature trends, precipitation trends, forecast charts, and a map.

3. Understand the Risk Score

The risk score is based on weather conditions such as high temperature, precipitation, and wind speed. Higher values can increase the risk level.

4. Save a Report

On the report page, click Save Report to store the current report in the database. Recently saved reports will appear on the Home page.

5. Read the Charts

The temperature chart shows forecasted daily high temperatures. The precipitation chart shows expected daily precipitation for the location.

6. Use the Map

The map shows the searched location using its latitude and longitude. You can zoom in or out to better understand the surrounding area.
