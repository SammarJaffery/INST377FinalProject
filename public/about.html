About This Project

Climate Risk Intelligence helps users search for a location and view simple, understandable weather and climate risk information.

The Problem

Many people are unaware of how climate change and extreme weather patterns may affect the places where they live or plan to move. This app helps make location-based climate information easier to understand.

Who This Helps

This tool can help residents, people planning to move, local governments, real estate professionals, and anyone interested in understanding climate risks in a specific area.

How Risk Is Calculated

The current version calculates a short-term risk score using a 7-day weather forecast. The score considers extreme heat, freezing temperatures, total precipitation, wind speed, and large temperature swings. Each factor adds risk points, and the total is converted into a Low, Medium, High, or Extreme rating.

Data Sources

This project uses weather and location data from Open-Meteo. Saved reports are stored in a Supabase database so users can view recent climate risk reports.
